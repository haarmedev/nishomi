Our Story

Say yes to conscious style.

Abaya reflects the personality, pride and sense of fashion of Arabic women which now has emerged to create a space of its own globally. Drawing inspiration from these aspects, NishOmi explores the untapped creativity and aesthetics that could add more to this piece of clothing. The aim is to capture the heritage, global appeal, and modesty through details, novel cuts, and styles.
